About Me

Hi, my name is Timothy Gangl. If you are looking for a tech-savvy Web Developer with a background in IT, then you have come to the right place. I recently completed an intense Full-Stack Web Developer Bootcamp through UC Davis. I also Have an A. A. S. in Network Administration, as well as being a certified CompTIA A+ IT technician.

I am a Sacramento area native that spent a little time out of state. I started my IT journey at Santa Fe Community College in the Information Technology program. After moving back to California I enrolled at MTI College where I received my A.S. in Network Administration. After ten years of doing side jobs fixing computers and setting up networks for friends and families, I felt it was time to do more. This is what has led me to my current Web Developer path.

I have always enjoyed learning how things work and understanding how each piece fits together to make it all work. I also enjoy creating things with a user in mind, whether it is my current role as a Category Merchandiser or in the realm of Web Development. I have always had enjoyed creating a functional and exciting UI/UX experience.

In my time away from Web Development I stay busy with a variety of interests. I have had a life long love affair with music and love anything involving it. I enjoy catching local shows and even find time to play a little music myself. Whether it's Jazz, Funk, Rock, or a fusion of these I enjoy playing music. I also enjoy getting out and going on hikes or bike rides with my wife or whatever outdoor adventure we can find.
